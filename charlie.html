Hello. Welcome. I am c/2nd Lieutenant Thu Vu and it is my absolute honor to be the Charlie Flight Commander for the 2019-2020 school year. I have been in AFJROTC for 3 years (going into my fourth) and besides my role in the operations squadron this year, I have had experience as a Public Affairs NCO and have also took the position as the 1st ever Education Commander in the unit. I have also been part of the Unarmed Fancy Drill Team, Stellarxplorers, Kitty Hawk, and as a Logistic representative. I usually don't have much free time but whenever I do, I like to bake, jam out to some music, play my violin, read, playing games, and hang out with my friends. If there are any questions, comments, or if you just want to be friends, please feel free to find me in the grinder during lunch or contact me through my email or phone number. Thank you for taking the time to visit my page :)
