Hello, everyone! I am c/2nd Lt. Marcus Velasquez and I am Bravo Flight’s Flight Commander for this year! I’ve been in this program since I was a freshman and I can honestly say that high school is much better because of it. Every day I walk into class (or join the Zoom call), I can’t help but feel the camaraderie and teamwork that unites us all together. It’s a warm, fuzzy feeling that can only be matched by a rainy Saturday night when you have no homework to take care of and all of the boys are online. The choice for me was simple: either do PE (boring, unoriginal, and 99% not PE), or join RO, learn some life skills, make friends, and do actual PE. Long story short, here I am, with an awesome flight and an equally awesome flight sergeant! In addition to this job, I am also a member of the StellarXplorers team, which is the closest you can get to Space Force JROTC. When I’m not in school, you can find me gaming, watching YouTube, playing my saxophone, or ignoring my alarm. My proverbial door is open at any time (except to those who own a dog named Jersey and like Weezer), so feel free to ask if you need help for things like studying knowledge, fixing your uniform, or preparing to join a team. I’m looking forward to working with you all this year!
